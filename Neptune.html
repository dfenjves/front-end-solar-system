Neptune

Neptune for dummies

  • It takes earth 164.8 earth years to rotate around the sun
  • The average surface temperature on Neptune is -214 degrees Celcius.
  • There are 14 moons around Neptune.
  • The climate on Neptune is very active. There are many storms that go through the planet.
  • Neptune is known as the Roman God of the sea.
  • There has only been one spacecraft that has flown past Neptune, the Voyager 2.
  • Neptune has the second largest gravity of all the planets, behind Jupiter.
