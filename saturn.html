Saturn

Saturn for dummies

  • Saturn is the sixth planet from the sun.
  • Saturn has the nickname of "the ringed planet".
  • It takes Saturn 29.4 earth years to rotate around the sun.
  • Saturn is the least dense planet, meaning it could float in water.
  • There are 150 moons orbitting around Saturn.
  • Saturn has been visited by four spacecrafts.
  • Saturn is the flattest planet.
